Leave Travel Allowance

  • As per the Rules, you can claim the LTA benefit only twice during the block of 4 years, current block is 2014-17 (Calender Year) . For this purpose, following condition should be satisfied: You should be on leave for 4 days. You should travel any where in India. During such travel you may have your family with you. LTA is part of your salary component.In case of journey performed by air, then you can get the LTA benefit for :Economy Air fair of National carrier by the shortest route or the amount spent which ever is less.In case of journey performed by rail, then you can get the LTA benefit for : A.C. first class rail fare by shortest route or amount spent which ever is less. International Travel is NOT ALLOWED for claiming LTA.
