HRA Exemption u/s 10(13A)

According to the notification issued by the Income tax department, now landlord PAN card is MUST to get tax exemption against HRA allowance. One will have to submit PAN card as a proof if he/she is applying for more than Rs.8,333 as monthly rent which means from now on, he/she needs to submit original rent receipts & agreement copy as proof of rent as well as PAN card copy of the landlord to get the tax exemption on HRA.
